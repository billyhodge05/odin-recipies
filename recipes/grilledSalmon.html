Grilled Salmon

Description

This is a delicious grilled salmon. Caught fresh in Alaska.

This grilled salmon recipe is sure to leave your family

craving for more

Ingredients

  • Salmon
  • Rice
  • Lemon Zest
  • Love

Steps

  1. Pour lemon zest on top of salmon
  2. Cook salmon on grill
  3. Cook rice in oven to perfection
  4. Add love
